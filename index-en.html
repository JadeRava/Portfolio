ABOUT US

The place where warm hospitality meets comfort, for a stay that warms the heart.

At Hotel Alba Rosa, every stay is a warm embrace that makes you feel at home. Located in a tranquil and picturesque setting, the hotel is lovingly and dedicatedly managed by our family, who have made hospitality a tradition. From breakfast made with fresh, local ingredients to personalized tips for exploring the area, everything is designed to provide you with an authentic and relaxing experience.

ACTIVITIES'

Unique experiences await you during your stay!

Gastronomy and Cuisine

Explore local and international flavors in our exclusive restaurants. Our chef will guide you on a unique culinary journey, with dishes prepared using fresh, high-quality ingredients. Don’t forget to try our fine wine selection in the lounge, perfect for a relaxing aperitif.

Outdoor Activities

For adventure enthusiasts, our hotel offers numerous outdoor activities. From guided hikes in the picturesque surroundings to bike rentals for exploring the area independently. Enjoy a day of golf on our private course or experience the thrill of a water sport in the Alassio sea.

Evening Entertainment

Evenings at our hotel are always special. Join our themed events, from live music nights to outdoor movie screenings under the stars. Or, relax on our panoramic terrace with breathtaking views, accompanied by background music and a cocktail crafted just for you.

Come discover a world of comfort, luxury, and fun, and make every moment unique.
